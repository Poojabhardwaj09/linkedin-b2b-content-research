I know it sounds "stupid" but if you want to go viral on LinkedIn...

Make your post look like this:

Short lines.

Then a longer thought that explains what you mean, gives context, and keeps people reading because it feels natural (and not like AI wrote it).

Then short again.

See what just happened? Your eyes just glided through it.

Here's my trick:

I've published 500+ LinkedIn posts. Every time, I squint my eyes until I can barely read the words. If all the "blocks" look the same size, I rewrite. If they vary like a rhythm, I know people will actually stop and read.

I only hit "Post" when it's beautiful.

Try it: zoom out on this post right now (CMD + minus). Long, medium, short, long, short, medium, short, long, medium, long, one word.

Lovely.

I tried this weird URL trick and got tons of new X followers 🧪

Swap your profile link with this secret link:

twitter[dot]com/intent/user?screen_name=YOURHANDLE

That's it!

Put it in your website footer, your email signature, your link-in-bio, etc. Anywhere you're sending people to your X (Twitter) page.

Here's why:

When someone clicks it, instead of just landing on your profile → they get a popup with a big fat Follow button. [See image]

We switched this everywhere at Wiz and the numbers went up pretty much immediately. 📈
