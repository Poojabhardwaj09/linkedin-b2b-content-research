No matter how behind they start. 
It’s hard to beat a man who never stops learning.
If you haven't spent more than 20 hours learning about AI, you will fall behind. 
Start this week, don't delay the first hour.



Insights:

* **The Competitor Hook:** Frames learning as an unfair competitive advantage that makes you "unbeatable."
* **Skill Quantification:** Shrinks a daunting subject (AI) into a manageable, non-intimidating "20-hour" milestone.
* **The Cost of Inaction:** Uses a "fear of falling behind" to create an immediate psychological gap that only action can fill.
* **Velocity Focus:** Prioritizes starting the "first hour" over planning the entire journey to kill procrastination.
* **Authority Positioning:** Uses short, punchy declarative statements to project certainty and high-status advice.
